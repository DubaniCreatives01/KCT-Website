Management Strategy

Property Management Unit (PMU)

The Property Management Unit of KCT Group is an internally managed unit with the primary goal of growing and improving cash flow to deliver quality earnings that underpin sustained growth for KBD Retail Properties. Property is our commodity, but people are our business, and relationships that we create sustain meaningful value for our business.

Our focus is on delivering sustainable growth added to the volatile local socio-economic environment of Khayelitsha. We fend off operational challenges and align practical strategies to enhance growth, making KBD Retail aspirations of a transformed KCT Mall upgrade and facelift more credible.

Tenant Mix & Occupation

KCT Mall remains 100% occupied by a fair tenant mix to Shoppers' delight. The separation of the two sections of KCT Mall allows Shoppers maximum flexibility and variety of commodity offerings.

Average Annual Growth (2022/2023) +9% Increase

Arrears recovery strategy through weekly reminders and demand notices has successfully stabilized revenue pipelines.

Operations Quality

Facilities Management Service Levels

Preventative maintenance, health and safety, deep cleaning, and fumigation operations governed by responsive service agreements:

Emergency Level

Unscheduled corrective activities whose failure could threaten the safety of Shoppers and Tenants.

Response Time: Immediate

Urgent Level

Unscheduled corrective activities where the problem does not pose immediate safety hazard but requires prompt actions.

Response Time: 1 workday / 3 days resolve

Routine Level

Minor day-to-day repairs, structural touching, and minor utilities maintenance jobs.

Response Time: Immediate / Day-to-day

Scheduled Level

Recurring preventative maintenance actions, major renovations, and event-related operations.

Response Time: As per schedules & plans
